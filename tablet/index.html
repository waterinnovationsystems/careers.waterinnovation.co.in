Service Engineer

Location: Bangalore

Job Description

We are seeking a skilled and motivated Service Engineer to handle installation, maintenance, and troubleshooting of Milton Roy dosing pumps and related water treatment systems. The candidate will be responsible for ensuring optimal performance, minimizing downtime, and delivering excellent technical support to clients.

Key Responsibilities

  • Installation & commissioning of Milton Roy dosing pumps (Maxroy, mRoy, PNP, PHL series)
  • Perform preventive & breakdown maintenance at customer sites
  • Diagnose and resolve mechanical, hydraulic, and electrical issues
  • Calibration and flow adjustment of dosing pumps
  • Provide technical support & training to clients/operators
  • Maintain service reports, logs, and documentation
  • Coordinate with sales team for AMC, spare parts & upgrades
  • Ensure compliance with safety standards and procedures
  • Handle field visits across multiple industrial sites

Technical Skills Required

  • Strong knowledge of: Dosing pumps (diaphragm, plunger type)
  • Flow rate calculation & calibration
  • Basic electrical troubleshooting
  • Familiarity with: Water treatment systems
  • Chemical dosing applications
  • Ability to read: P&ID diagrams, Technical manuals

Qualifications

  • Diploma / BE / B.Tech in Mechanical / Electrical / Instrumentation Engineering

Experience

  • 1 to 4 years (Freshers with strong technical knowledge can apply)
  • Experience in water treatment / pump industry preferred
